Facts

USA alleges Iran of sabotaging of oil freighers in May and June 2019.

   In May 2019, USA reports indicated naval transport could be at risk in the Persian Gulf and specifically warned Iran not to strike. The reports turned out to be correct as a handful of cargo ships were damaged in US-controlled waters at the U. A. Emirates.

   US Officials pointed to Iran as the culprit and immediately deployed more troops and materials to the region. A few weeks after (June), two other oil tankers were attacked

Nuclear Program

    In 2008, Iran had denied having a nuclear weapons program. It explained its occupation with Urianium with the development of nuclear powerplants and the according technology. Closer to 2019, suspicions turned out to be legitimate.

   To the USA, it has become increasingly clear that Iran is looking for stronger regional control and - most importantly - increased control over the oil trade. Becuase of the suspicion the USA 'aired' drone reconnaissance programs.

Drone reconnaissance | "Obliteration like you've never seen before"

    The tension it caused with Iraq reached a peak in 2011, when an Iranian cyberwarfare department took control of a USA drone and stole it for close technological inspection.

   Another peak was reached in June 2019, when a US drone was shot by a land-to-air missile. The US army allegedly sought immediate retaliation, but president Trump had halted any engagements 10 minutes prior to mission launch; he sought to avoid war with Iran. In stead, he strongly increased sanctions, deployed B-52 bombers in the region and informed Iran that conflict would mean "Obliteration like you've never seen before".

Russia's meddling

   During the Syria crisis, it became apparent that Russia seeks control over the Middle East. The trend was reiterated earlier this year, when the country supplied Turkey with a highly capable missile system despite US' strong resistance. It is unclear whether Russia seeks to provide the same S-400 missile system to Iran, but both rumours and motive are present.

   Russia's interest in Iran stems from the country's positon as bastion against full US control over the Middle East. As we noticed in the Gulf War, Iran is a large country with much potential. The tensions between USA and Iran have high stakes and should be followed carefully.
