Chocolate-Banana cake

Chocolate-Banana Cake A different take on your favorite type of cake

•  2 cups All-purpose flour
•  1 cup cocao powder
•  1 teaspoon salt
•  2 cups granulated sugar
•  2 eggs
•  1 1/2 bananas
•  1/2 teaspoon baking soda
•  1 3/4 cup Liquids ~ milk, water or coffee
•  80 grams butter
•  20 grams coconut butter (optional)

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 185 C / 355 Fahrenheit


2. Mix 2/5 cup flour, butter and hot liquid


3. Add other ingredients


4. Place in middle of oven for 28-38 minutes*
*duration determines firmness
